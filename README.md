# KRIDHA Engineering & Services

KRIDHA Engineering & Services is a professionally managed engineering company delivering integrated solutions in Electrical Systems, Air Conditioning (HVAC), and Fire Safety serving residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

## Our Expertise
We specialize in end-to-end services — from product sales and system design to installation, commissioning, and maintenance — ensuring safe, efficient, and high-performance infrastructure.

## Core Services
- **Electrical Systems**: LT & HT installations, panels, distribution, and structured cabling.
- **HVAC Systems**: Energy-efficient cooling, VRV/VRF systems, and centralized AC.
- **Fire Safety**: Alarm & detection, hydrant & sprinkler systems, and compliance.
